Get to know me

Ola!

My name is Alex Theo,

I'm an 18 yo Photographer and I am very glad to see you here :).

Photography is for me a way to escape my reality while being present in the very moment. I think it's fascinating how we are able to capture the world from our unique viewpoint and freeze it in time, and so I take Photography as a privilege that I'm very honoured to have.

I take all kinds of photos, from Portrait to Street Photography and Architecture, mainly because I'm just at the beginning of my career and like to experiment. I work with a Canon 500D and different editing programms. Take a look around and if you think my work is cool and are interested in any business with me - just hit me up ;-).
